Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 9, 2025

This privacy policy explains how the Eparty Event app (“the App”) handles personal information. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to being transparent about our practices.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the App, we collect the following information:

  • User Identity: Full name and phone number you enter when setting up your identity in the app.
  • Event Data: Event names, descriptions, dates, and other details you input.
  • Invitee Information: Full names and phone numbers of people you invite to events.

2. How Information Is Used

All personal data is stored locally on your device only. The App does not send, transmit, or back up this information to any remote server or cloud service. As developers, we do not have access to any of your personal or event-related data.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

You can edit or delete any data within the app at any time. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data from your device.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party SDKs or analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase). However, Google Play or your device's operating system may collect anonymized data about app usage in accordance with their policies.

5. No Internet Connection Required

The App is designed to function offline and does not require an internet connection for its core features. No user data is transmitted over the internet by the App itself.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or store any personal information from children.
